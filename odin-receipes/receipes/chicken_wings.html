Chicken Wings

Description

These crispy, flavorful chicken wings are perfect for any gathering. Coated in a tangy sauce, they are a hit at parties and family meals alike!

Ingredients

  • 12 chicken wings
  • ½ cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ¼ cup butter, melted
  • ½ cup hot sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Mix flour, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Coat chicken wings with the mixture.
  4. Bake wings for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Melt butter and stir in hot sauce.
  6. Toss baked wings in the sauce.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
