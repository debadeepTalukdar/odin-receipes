Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish consisting of layers of pasta, cheese, and meat sauce, baked to perfection. A family favorite for gatherings and dinners!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain.
  3. Melt butter, cook onion and garlic, then stir in flour.
  4. Add chicken broth, milk, and salt.
  5. Stir in 2 cups mozzarella and 1/4 cup Parmesan.
  6. Layer noodles, sauce, spinach, and cheese.
  7. Bake until golden brown and bubbly.
  8. Enjoy!
